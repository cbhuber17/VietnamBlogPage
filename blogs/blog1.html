Vietnam Trip: October 2014

This was my first trip to Vietnam! I came on a bus with a buddy from Cambodia and we stayed in the Phạm Ngũ Lão / Bùi Viện region in distrct 1 of Ho Chi Minh city.

Here you can enjoy delicious Vietnamese foods, such as deep fried spring rolls (Chả giò / nem rán). Yummy!

This was such an amazing trip that I came back to Vietnam many more times after this trip!

Beautiful country and lovely people!
