Vietnam Trip: November 2019

This was my fourth trip to Vietnam! This time I tried staying in local apartments rather than hotels where I could. I went to the touristy island Phú Quốc to enjoy some island time!

The seafood is incredibly fresh and delicious. Gotta love the snails with tamarind sauce!

Many of the temples have the same appearance in Buddhist regions. I always love the relaxing smell of incense whenever I visit a temple.

The seafood is really ngon quá! (delicious!)
